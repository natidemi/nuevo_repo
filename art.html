Stanley Donwood

Stanley Donwood is an artist and writer who has collaborated with Radiohead since the release of their single 'My Iron Lung' in 1994. Having met frontman Thom Yorke at the University of Exeter, he has gone on to create all the band's artwork to date.

I really love it when you get that strange combination of feelings that play against each other.

Stanley Donwood
Thom & Stanley

London, 2014
